Meet Barbeaux. Thirsty?

Let's take the bartender out of the bar. Simply browse to whichever of our sixteen drinks suits your fancy and Barbeux will serve it up in a matter of seconds. We can make a drink faster, more precisely, and more pleasantly than a real human bartender could ever hope to do.

Mechanical robustness.

The mechanical system of Barbeaux is designed to be easily assembled and consists of five main components: a main structure, 6 bottle holders, a constantly rotating storage disc where users can grab their drinks from, a cup transporter, and a cup pusher that delivers a full cup onto the storage disc. The mechanical system is a perfect fit for making drinks quickly.

Electrical precision.

Barbeaux can pour a drink from any combination of six bottles with less than five milliliters of error. All using electronics that cost less than $20. Oh, and it's waterproof. See how we did it.

Software simplicity.

A software interface doesn't have to be dry. Barbeux lets you leisurely surf through twelve sumptuous drinks, reading bios and understanding the ingredients. The software interface really makes Barbeaux your servant. See for yourself.
